Navigate to Symbol

Navigate to a symbol by symbol name.

Alt+Command+O

1. Navigate to symbol
   Navigate to the 'detailDescriptionLabel' property of DetailViewController class.

2. Use CamelHumps to navigate
   Navigate to the application:didFinishLaunchingWithOptions: method using CamelHumps (type "adf")

3. Note that middle matching and wildcards also work when searching symbols.
   Example searches: "didFinish" or "finish" for the application:didFinishLaunchingWithOptions: method.
